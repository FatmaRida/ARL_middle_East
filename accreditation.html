Accreditation

We are an internationally accredited as certification body form The Egyptian Accreditation Council EGAC. and agent for a certification body accredited from :

  • The German Accreditation (DAKKS).
  • American Accreditation (IAS).

And registered with many Egyptian entities and institutions, including:

  • The Egyptian Organization for Standards & Quality (EOS).
  • EGYPTIAN GENERAL PETROLEUM CORPORATION (EGPC).
  • Industrial Modernization Centre (IMC).
